What is an expat?

    An expatriate, or 'expat' for short, is someone who is living outside their native country. If you're currently living abroad, you are an expat and this site is for you!

Who can access my photos?

    Everyone! Expat's Journey is a public photo sharing site but if you're looking for a more private way to share your expat related photos, we have a private sharing option in the works.
